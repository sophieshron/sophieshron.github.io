MOFAD
THURS, FEB 27
7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Join playwright Jaime Sunwoo for a discussion about the history of SPAM, the canned meat, in the Asia-Pacific and its connection to war, class, and nostalgia.

Sunwoo will share her process, and reveal why she uses SPAM as a metaphor for the Asian American experience.
